When investing, weigh: YIELD (higher APY = faster growth), RISK (exploits, depegs, slashing), LIQUIDITY (can you exit quickly?), and DIVERSIFICATION (don't concentrate).

   Lend ETH on Aave V3. Borrowers pay interest. $40B+ TVL, extensively audited.

#2 Lido wstETH [MEDIUM risk, 3-4% APY, instant via DEX]

#4 Rocket Pool rETH [MEDIUM risk, ~3.1% APY, instant via DEX]
